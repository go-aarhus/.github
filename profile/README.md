# Go Aarhus 🐹

Welcome to the Go Aarhus community — a meetup group for Go enthusiasts in and around Aarhus, Denmark.

We get together to share knowledge, explore the Go ecosystem, and connect with other Gophers in the area. Whether you are a seasoned Go developer or just getting started, you are welcome here.

## What we do

- Regular meetups with talks, demos, and discussions
- A mix of deep dives, lightning talks, and open conversations about Go
- A friendly space to ask questions, share projects, and geek out over the language

## Get involved

**Slack** — Join the [`#goaarhus`](https://gophers.slack.com/archives/CNRDQMCSY) channel in the Gophers Slack workspace for day-to-day conversation, announcements, and meetup coordination. *Not yet in the Gophers Slack? Get an invite at [invite.slack.golangbridge.org](https://invite.slack.golangbridge.org).*

**LinkedIn** — Follow us for event announcements and meetup highlights: [go-aarhus](https://www.linkedin.com/company/go-aarhus)

## Want to speak or suggest a topic?

We are always looking for speakers and topic ideas. If you have something you would like to present — a project, a library, a war story, or a deep dive on a language feature — reach out to us on Slack or open an issue in this repository.

## Code of Conduct

We follow the [Go Community Code of Conduct](https://go.dev/conduct). Be kind, be respectful, and make this a welcoming space for everyone.
